Campaigner 🔧

Diplomats

As a Campaigner (ENFP), you are a bold and practical experimenter, master of all kinds of tools. You have a natural mechanical curiosity and love to take things apart to see how they work. You are independent and adaptable, often reacting quickly to change and crisis.

You are a person of few words, preferring action over conversation. You value your freedom and need space to pursue your own interests. You are logical and rational, but you also have a spontaneous streak that can surprise those around you.

You may struggle with long-term planning and emotional commitment, preferring to live in the moment. You can appear insensitive or detached to more emotional types. Learning to communicate your feelings and consider the future consequences of your actions will help you build stronger relationships.
