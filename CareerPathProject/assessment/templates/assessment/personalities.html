Personality types

"The privilege of a lifetime is to become who you truly are." — Carl Jung

Explore your personality by taking our test

ANALYSTS

STRATEGIC THINKERS & VISIONARIES

Rational and impartial, Analysts enjoy intellectual pursuits and excel at strategic thinking.

INTJ / INTJ-T

ARCHITECT

Visionary Strategist

Architects are visionary strategists who approach every situation with depth, foresight, and precision. They naturally craft long-term plans and excel at turning complex ideas into clear, structured solutions.

Strategic Visionary Structured
INTP / INTP-T

LOGICIAN

Analytical Explorer

Logicians are analytical explorers who thrive on understanding how things work. Their inventive minds constantly seek patterns, theories, and new possibilities, making them pioneers of original thought.

Analytical Inventive Theoretical
ENTJ / ENTJ-T

COMMANDER

Dynamic Trailblazer

Commanders are dynamic trailblazers with a natural talent for leadership. They take charge confidently, create well-defined goals, and inspire teams to achieve ambitious results with efficiency and clarity.

Leader Dynamic Efficient
ENTP / ENTP-T

DEBATER

Energetic Innovator

Debaters are energetic innovators who enjoy questioning norms and exploring different angles of an idea. Their sharp intellect and fearless curiosity make them exceptional problem-solvers in fast-moving environments.

Debater Curious Innovative
🌟 DIPLOMATS

EMPATHETIC IDEALISTS & MOTIVATORS

Empathetic, diplomatic, and passionate idealists who inspire and guide others toward meaningful change.

INFJ / INFJ-T

ADVOCATE

Thoughtful Visionary

Advocates are thoughtful visionaries with a deep sense of purpose. They combine quiet strength with heartfelt conviction, often guiding others with compassion, insight, and unwavering idealism.

👁️ Visionary ❤️ Compassionate ⭐ Idealistic
INFP / INFP-T

MEDIATOR

Gentle Dreamer

Mediators are gentle dreamers driven by empathy and creativity. Their warm, genuine nature inspires them to support meaningful causes and uplift the people around them with kindness.

🕊️ Gentle 🎨 Creative 💝 Empathetic
ENFJ / ENFJ-T

PROTAGONIST

Natural Motivator

Protagonists are natural motivators who lead with passion and sincerity. Their ability to connect deeply with others makes them influential guides who spark confidence and positive change wherever they go.

🚀 Motivator 🔥 Passionate 📢 Influential
ENFP / ENFP-T

CAMPAIGNER

Lively Spirit

Campaigners are lively and imaginative spirits who radiate optimism. Their boundless creativity and social warmth help them brighten any space and find excitement in every new possibility.

☀️ Optimistic 🌈 Imaginative 👥 Social
⚡ SENTINELS

PRACTICAL GUARDIANS & ORGANIZERS

Practical, orderly, and reliable guardians who value structure and bring stability to their communities.

ISTJ / ISTJ-T

LOGISTICIAN

Grounded Individual

Logisticians are grounded, detail-oriented individuals who value clarity and structure. Their strong sense of responsibility and steady commitment make them dependable in any situation.

🔍 Detail-oriented 🏔️ Grounded 🛡️ Dependable
ISFJ / ISFJ-T

DEFENDER

Compassionate Caretaker

Defenders are compassionate caretakers who quietly support and safeguard the people they value. Their loyalty, patience, and nurturing nature create a comforting presence for everyone around them.

🤗 Caretaker 💎 Loyal 🛡️ Protective
ESTJ / ESTJ-T

EXECUTIVE

Organized Leader

Executives are organized, confident leaders who excel at bringing plans to life. Their strong decision-making and structured approach help teams stay focused, efficient, and productive.

📋 Organized ⚡ Decisive ⚙️ Productive
ESFJ / ESFJ-T

CONSUL

Warm-hearted Individual

Consultants are warm-hearted and socially attuned individuals who enjoy creating harmony. Their supportive nature and enthusiasm for helping others make them valued members of every community.

👥 Social 🤝 Supportive 🎵 Harmonious
🚀 EXPLORERS

SPONTANEOUS INNOVATORS & PERFORMERS

Spontaneous, energetic, and enthusiastic individuals who thrive in dynamic environments and embrace new experiences.

ISTP / ISTP-T

VIRTUOSO

Practical Innovator

Virtuosos are practical innovators who love working with their hands and discovering how things function. They are calm, adaptable, and skilled at finding quick, effective solutions in unexpected situations.

🔧 Practical 🌊 Adaptable ⚡ Quick
ISFP / ISFP-T

ADVENTURER

Gentle Creative

Adventurers are gentle creatives with a natural flair for expression. They move through life with curiosity and openness, constantly seeking new experiences and ways to appreciate beauty in the world around them.

🎨 Creative 🔍 Curious ✨ Aesthetic
ESTP / ESTP-T

ENTREPRENEUR

Bold Individual

Entrepreneurs are bold, fast-thinking individuals who thrive in action-oriented environments. Their sharp instincts and lively energy make them masters at seizing opportunities and keeping life exciting.

💪 Bold ⚡ Energetic 🎯 Opportunistic
ESFP / ESFP-T

ENTERTAINER

Vibrant Personality

Entertainers are vibrant, joyful personalities who bring light and fun wherever they go. Their spontaneity and genuine warmth make every moment lively and memorable for the people around them.

😊 Joyful 🌟 Vibrant 💫 Memorable
